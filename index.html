Yoga timetable

Slow Flow Yoga

A physically accessible class focused on breathing and stretching. A great slow, strong, and calm yoga practice to start your week!

Yoga

Increase your hip and shoulder mobility with these intermediate vinyasa-style flows: breathe, sweat, balance.

Yoga Drills

A gym-style workout, incorporating repetitive motions into your yoga practice, to build endurance, work your deep core, and reach your yoga asana goals!

Climbing Fitness

Fun, repetitive muscle isolation exercises focused on deep core stability to build strength, improve coordination, and support dynamic climbing movements.

Budokon

Budokon fuses martial arts, calisthenics, animal mobility, and yoga into one amazing full mind-body workout. Build a sweat and increase your core stability, and body awareness with this steady, dynamic, and strong flow.
