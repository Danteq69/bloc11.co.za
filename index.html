Contribute to Julia Chen's Medical Bills

Our dear friend Julia Chen passed away after a climbing accident. We are trying to help the family pay for the medical bills associated with her passing by raising a sum of R125 000, which will be fully donated to the family. Bloc 11 will cover the costs of transactions and associated bank fees, so the full donated sum will be passed onto the family. Email us at bloc.eleven@gmail.com for bank details, if you prefer to EFT.
